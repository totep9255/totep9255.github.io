About

Mira works at the point where a brand's idea becomes something you can actually hold, click, or walk past — identity systems, editorial layouts, and the odd piece of signage.

The studio takes on a small number of projects each year, favouring clients with a clear point of view over ones needing a rescue. Most work starts with type, not a mood board.
